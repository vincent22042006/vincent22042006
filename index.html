Qui suis-je?

Vincent Leonardon-Bougault

Currently a second-year student pursuing a Bachelor’s degree in Marketing and Sales (BUT Techniques de Commercialisation) at IUT Montpellier, I have gained valuable hands-on experience through a variety of roles in the hospitality sector. Working in a casual restaurant, an ice cream shop, and within the luxury brasserie of the Plage Palace Hotel in Palavas has allowed me to adapt to very different environments, develop strong customer-facing skills, and build a solid sense of efficiency, organisation, and professionalism.

I regularly practice swimming and padel, activities that strengthen my sense of discipline, and teamwork. At the same time, music plays an important role in my daily life, and I aim to integrate this artistic dimension into my future café by hosting DJ sessions and welcoming artists, with the goal of creating a lively, welcoming, and musically inspired space. Curious, motivated, and customer-oriented, I am constantly seeking new opportunities to expand my experience and grow in a dynamic environment.

Completed Projects and Skills

Excel

My academic and personal experiences have enabled me to develop strong proficiency in Excel. I am comfortable using a wide range of formulas, creating pivot tables, and performing structured data analysis—skills I regularly apply within the Student Union (BDE). This ease with spreadsheets makes me rigorous, autonomous, and responsive when it comes to meeting analysis or reporting needs.

Canva

Canva is the tool I have used the most, both in my studies and in my community projects. I am proficient in the main layout, typography, and graphic design features, which allows me to create modern, clear, and consistent visuals. Thanks to this regular practice, I can easily produce materials adapted to different formats while respecting a visual identity and responding quickly to communication needs.

HTML, CSS et GIMP

JI have developed solid foundations in HTML and CSS, which I use to design and structure web pages, organize content, and create clean, clear, and functional layouts. These skills give me real autonomy in developing digital projects and are directly aligned with my professional goals, particularly the creation of the website for my future café and its associated brand. In parallel, through my personal and associative projects, I have gained strong proficiency in GIMP, which I use for image retouching and visual enhancement (selection tools, layer management, corrections, and adjustments). This combination of web development and graphic design allows me to produce coherent, communication-oriented content with rigor, creativity, and precision.

Marketing of craft beer

As part of a cross-disciplinary project involving students in Biological Engineering and Business/Marketing (Techniques de Commercialisation), I took part in the marketing of a craft beer—a project that our team won. I contributed to the pricing strategy, communication, and the creation of visual materials (using Canva and GIMP), as well as to the macro-environment analysis to identify market trends and opportunities. I also helped define the product strategy, which strengthened my interest in product design and logistics. This project allowed me to combine rigor, creativity, and strategic thinking, while developing a global view of launching an artisanal product.

Completed internships

I completed my first internship in April 2024 at Eyes Up Factory, a company specializing in large-scale event signage. During this experience, I learned to operate industrial machines, carry out various hands-on tasks, and manage part of the logistics process. Following this internship, the company hired me on a temporary basis to continue working in its workshops. My second internship took place at Hervé Bancel Immobilier, within both the agency and the property management (syndic) department. I discovered the fundamentals of property management, tenant relations, and the legal aspects related to real estate, thanks to the guidance and expertise of the company’s director. I was also trained in carrying out basic technical maintenance tasks.

World record in indoor rowing

In 2023, I took part in a collective challenge aimed at breaking a world record: covering one million meters with 80 participants in under 15 hours. This challenge evolved into a solidarity project in support of La Ronde de l’Espoir, raising €23,000. This experience had a strong impact on me, as it taught me the power of teamwork, collective commitment, and the ability to push beyond limits in pursuit of a meaningful goal. Through this project, I developed a strong sense of initiative and a motivation to carry forward ambitious, purpose-driven initiatives.

Commercial Communication

As part of my studies in the Bachelor’s degree in Marketing and Sales (BUT Techniques de Commercialisation), I completed a fictional project as a Communication Officer for the company Eyes Up. I analysed competitors’ posters and brochures, as well as the company’s initial communication materials, in order to identify their strengths and weaknesses. This analysis enabled me to design a more effective poster and brochure for commercial communication, taking into account visual, marketing, and strategic considerations.

Community Involvement

Since September, I have been actively involved in the association Terre de Mission Haïti, where I have contributed to the development of concrete projects combining communication, event management, and coordination. I notably co-organised a boxing gala in Montpellier, during which I was responsible for the DJ performance and contributed to the overall organisation of the event. This experience allowed me to gain exposure to various aspects of event production, including sound atmosphere management, an introduction to lighting, and technical coordination. In addition, my colleague and I worked on redesigning the association’s website in order to improve its visibility and communication. These projects taught me how to work under real constraints, collaborate effectively within a team, and understand the importance of image, rhythm, and messaging in a practical, real-world context.
